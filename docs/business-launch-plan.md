# Business Launch Plan

## Validate before polishing

Talk to 5 to 10 agents and ask:

- How do you track clients today?
- What is the hardest part of managing leads?
- What makes deals slip through the cracks?
- Would reminders and a simple pipeline help daily work?

## Early access goal

Before full launch, get 3 to 5 agents to say yes to trying the product.

Pitch:

`Simple CRM for property agents to track clients, deals, and follow-ups without spreadsheets.`

## Launch channels

1. Myanmar real estate Facebook groups
2. Direct outreach to agents
3. Personal network and referrals

## Pricing

- Free: 10 leads
- $5/month: solo agent
- $10/month: small team

## Retention levers

- daily tasks on first screen
- visible overdue reminders
- sample demo data for onboarding
- fast setup in under 10 minutes

## Revenue truth

Passive income only happens after:

- agents use it every day
- churn is low
- onboarding becomes repeatable

At the start, expect hands-on selling and support.
